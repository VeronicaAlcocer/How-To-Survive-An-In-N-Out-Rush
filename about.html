ABOUT THE CREATORS

Why, hello there

I'm Veronica. I created this website because it was an assigned project. My class was told to code a "Survial Guide" of some kind, while meeting some criteria. I decided to create the most millennial Californian concept I could think of: a website dedicated to our beloved fast food chain. I hope browsing this site made you laugh a little. If you thought it was super corny or cheesy, I will be flattered. This website was not meant to be taken seriously.

If you want to know a little more about me, I am a student at Academy of Art University. I'm earning a BFA in Advertising, and hope to work in an agency as a copywriter after graduating. I also love corgis! It's what I wrote about for my very first HTML assignment.
